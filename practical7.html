Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | Former President

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs.

Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna Award (1997)
  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle
  • Key figure in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Inspiring teacher and author

Contact / Legacy

Known for inspiring millions of students through his speeches and books. His vision continues to guide India's youth.
